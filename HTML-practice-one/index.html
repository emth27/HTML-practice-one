Anchor

The anchor element is what is used to create links. Links are used to link within the same webpage, website, or link to other websites. When you use the target attribute with the value of "blank", the link opens up in a new tab on the browser you are using. If you want to learn more about links, MDN is a great resource and has examples to help you understand how links work and more.

How to bake chocolate chip cookies:

  1. Preheat Oven:
    • Preheat your oven to the temperature specified on the packaging (usually around 350°F or 175°C).
  2. Open the Dough Package:
    • Open the package of ready-to-bake chocolate chip cookie dough.
  3. Slice or Scoop Dough:
    • Depending on the packaging, either slice the cookie dough into individual rounds or use a cookie scoop to portion the dough.
  4. Place on Baking Sheet:
    • Arrange the cookie dough portions on an ungreased or parchment-lined baking sheet, leaving some space between each.
  5. Bake:
    • Follow the recommended baking time and temperature provided on the package. It's typically around 10-12 minutes, but this can vary.
  6. Cool:
    • Allow the baked cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. Enjoy:
    • Once cooled, your ready-to-bake chocolate chip cookies are ready to be enjoyed!
